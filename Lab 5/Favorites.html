My Favorites

Music

I enjoy listening to a variety of genres, particularly rap, hip-hop, and OPM (Original Pilipino Music). Some of my favorite artists include:

  • Shanti Dope – known for his unique style and storytelling in rap.
  • Gloc-9 – a legendary Filipino rapper with powerful lyrics.
  • Al James – creative and melodic tracks that inspire me.

Food

Food is another passion of mine. Here are some dishes I particularly enjoy:

  • Adobo – a classic Filipino comfort food.
  • Sinigang – tangy and flavorful, perfect for any day.
  • Bulalo – hearty and rich, especially on colder days.

Hobbies

During my free time, I like to engage in activities that keep me active and creative:

  • Folk dancing – performing traditional dances and connecting with culture.
  • Composing music – creating original tracks and lyrics.
  • Playing video games – for strategy, fun, and relaxation.

These favorites reflect my personality, interests, and the things that motivate me every day.
