🎬 My Favorite Movies

Here are some of my all-time favorite movies. I enjoy films that are full of style, action, comedy, and great storytelling.

  • Scott Pilgrim vs. the World — Action, comedy, music, and video-game vibes all in one.
  • Django Unchained — A bold western with action, revenge, and powerful storytelling.
  • Kill Bill Vol. 1 & 2 — Quentin Tarantino’s martial arts revenge epic (similar style to Django).
  • Deadpool — Action + comedy with a self-aware, comic-inspired vibe (similar to Scott Pilgrim).
  • Baby Driver — Stylish action film with music at its core (great mix of beats + visuals).
  • The Hateful Eight — Another Tarantino western with sharp dialogue and tension.
  • Kick-Ass — A fun, comic-style action-comedy with an edgy twist (same vibe as Scott Pilgrim).

These movies inspire me with their energy, creativity, and unique storytelling styles.
