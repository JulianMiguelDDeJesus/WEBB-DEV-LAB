About TWICE

TWICE is a nine-member girl group formed by JYP Entertainment in 2015 through the survival show *Sixteen*. Known for their catchy songs, synchronized choreography, and bright concepts, TWICE quickly rose to become one of the most loved K-pop groups globally. Their fandom, ONCE, is among the most dedicated fanbases, supporting the group through every era and milestone.

From their breakthrough hit "Cheer Up" to "TT," "Fancy," and "I Can’t Stop Me," TWICE has delivered unforgettable music and performances. They are celebrated not only for their stage presence, but also for their unique personalities and strong bond with fans.

✨ Why I Love TWICE ✨

TWICE is more than just music — they represent joy, energy, and togetherness. Each member brings something unique to the stage: Nayeon’s charisma, Jeongyeon’s stability, Momo’s incredible dance, Sana’s charm, Jihyo’s powerful vocals, Mina’s grace, Dahyun’s humor, Chaeyoung’s artistry, and Tzuyu’s elegance. Together, they form a group that continues to win hearts worldwide.
