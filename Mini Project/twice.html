About TWICE

TWICE is a nine-member girl group formed by JYP Entertainment in 2015. Known for their catchy songs, choreography, and cheerful concepts, TWICE has grown into one of K-pop’s most successful groups.

✨ Why TWICE is Special ✨

Each member of TWICE brings unique charm and talent: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. Together, they’ve created a legacy of joy and energy in K-pop.
