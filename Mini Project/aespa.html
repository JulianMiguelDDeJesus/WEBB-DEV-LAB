About aespa

aespa debuted in 2020 under SM Entertainment with the members Karina, Winter, Giselle, and Ningning. They stand out for their virtual world concept, blending real and AI avatars into storytelling.

With hits like "Black Mamba," "Next Level," and "Savage," aespa has become one of K-pop’s most unique groups.
