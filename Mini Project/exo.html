About EXO

EXO is a South Korean-Chinese boy group formed by SM Entertainment in 2012. Originally with 12 members, EXO has become one of the most iconic K-pop groups of the decade. Known for their impressive vocals, synchronized choreography, and diverse concepts, EXO has dominated charts in Korea and beyond.

With songs like "Growl," "Call Me Baby," and "Love Shot," EXO has solidified their reputation as trendsetters in the industry. Their fandom, EXO-L, continues to passionately support them, making them one of the longest-standing successful groups in K-pop history.
