About BTS

BTS, also known as Bangtan Sonyeondan, debuted in 2013 and grew into one of the most influential K-pop groups worldwide. They blend pop, hip-hop, and R&B while addressing themes like mental health, self-love, and social issues.

Their fandom, ARMY, supports their global impact — from music to humanitarian work. BTS continues inspiring millions globally.
