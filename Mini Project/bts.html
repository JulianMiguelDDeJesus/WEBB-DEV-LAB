About BTS

BTS, also known as Bangtan Sonyeondan or Bulletproof Boy Scouts, is a seven-member group formed by BigHit Entertainment. Since their debut in 2013, BTS has become one of the most influential K-pop groups in the world. Their music blends pop, hip-hop, and R&B, often addressing themes like mental health, self-love, and social issues.

Known for their synchronized performances, heartfelt lyrics, and global campaigns, BTS has built an enormous fan base called ARMY. Their contributions extend beyond music, as they’ve spoken at the United Nations and collaborated with UNICEF for the "Love Myself" campaign. BTS has broken countless records and continues to inspire fans worldwide.
