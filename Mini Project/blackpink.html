About BLACKPINK

BLACKPINK is a four-member girl group under YG Entertainment: Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. Debuting in 2016, they quickly became a global sensation with their unique sound, style, and presence.

With hits like "DDU-DU DDU-DU," "Kill This Love," and "Pink Venom," BLACKPINK is one of the most streamed girl groups globally. Their fandom, BLINK, continues to push them to the top of worldwide charts.
